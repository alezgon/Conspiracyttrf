Observational Fragment — Umbra

In psychological literature, Umbra refers to the shadowed aspects of the psyche. Traits denied, suppressed, or unexamined.

In practice, the phenomenon behaves less metaphorically. Awareness alters behavior. Observation strengthens manifestation.

Do not name it aloud.

Once separated, the subject deteriorates.

The body remains. Something else leaves.

Lucian.
